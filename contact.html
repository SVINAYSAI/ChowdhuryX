Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation take?

Most projects are completed within 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Simple chatbots can be ready in 2 weeks, while custom AI solutions may take 6-12 weeks.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes! All our solutions include ongoing support and maintenance. We offer 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, and continuous improvements as part of our service.

Can I integrate with existing systems?

Absolutely. Our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing CRM, ERP, and other business systems through APIs and custom connectors.

Security is our top priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption, follow SOC 2 compliance standards, and never share your data with third parties.
